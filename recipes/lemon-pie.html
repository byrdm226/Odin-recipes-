Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 4 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 2/3 cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F(200 degress Celcius)
  2. Lightly grease a 10-inch pie plate. Combine lemon juice, sugar, eggs, coconut, flour, and milk in a large bowl. Stir until well blended and pour mixture into pie dish
  3. Bake in the pre-heated oven until center is set but slightly jiggly, about 35 minutes. Let Pie cool completely before slicing
